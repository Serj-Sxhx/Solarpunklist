☀︎ We fund builders through grants and fellowships.

### ~/ World Payment Experiment

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6691010455371864d09b8800/69e738fbc26d35adbda3b185_WORLD-ASCII.png)

A partnership with World to test whether crypto-native payments, identity, and mobile coordination tools could support a community of 750 people over a full month at Edge City Patagonia.

‍

Around 500 participants (70% of the village) downloaded World App, 33 local merchants in San Martín de los Andes were onboarded, and over $44,000 flowed through the system.

~/ Community Currency

Governance

### ~/ Community Currency

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6691010455371864d09b8800/69d3c3b822687ef9c69a022f_Frame%201000002974.png)

A RadicalxChange experiment in alternative economic systems. Hundreds of participants traded 'edges' for everything from dinner parties and airport rides to photography and coding help. By month's end, each edge had appreciated from $0.20 to an estimated $1-10 through network effects. Later iterations introduced quadratic scoring, physical markets, and fiscal stimulus programs, advancing the experiment toward a genuine community banking system.

~/ Vasocomputation

Research

### ~/ Vasocomputation

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6691010455371864d09b8800/69e739089d00eeb71a91db33_VASOCOMPUTATION-ASCII.png)

A theory by neuroscience researcher Michael Edward Johnson proposing that the brain's vascular system is an active computational layer. Blood vessel smooth muscle cells store predictions and unresolved experiences as physical tension through a "latch-bridge" mechanism, offering a biological explanation for how trauma gets held in the body and how contemplative practices can release it. We are running active experiments on vasocomputation across our popup villages.

~/ Esmeralda

New Town

### ~/ Esmeralda

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6691010455371864d09b8800/69d3c3f0f5fd022a57be63c9_workisland-87characters.png)

A permanent walkable village taking shape in the hills of Cloverdale, 90 minutes north of San Francisco. Inspired by what emerged from our popup villages, Esmeralda is designed as a multigenerational neighborhood featuring up to 605 residences, an extraordinary hotel, a community piazza, event spaces, and a publicly accessible network of trails, playgrounds, and sports fields, all set against views of the Mayacamas Mountains.

~/ Constellation

Startup

### ~/ Constellation

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6691010455371864d09b8800/69d3c3fa3fe627ef7992caf5_Frame%201000002977.png)

Founded by Stanford neuroscientist Avery Krieger during the 2025 Long Journey Residency at Edge Esmeralda. Constellation builds AI foundation models trained on neural, physiological, and behavioral data to treat mental illness and power brain-computer interfaces. During the residency, Avery collected one of the largest multimodal neural datasets of its kind from village participants. The company has since raised $10M.
